\section{Error Between Two Sinusoidal Signals}
\label{Ap_Err2S}

The motion equation of an assumed sinusoidal tremor with amplitude, $X_1$, and angular frequency, $\omega$, is represented by
\begin{equation}
x_1(t)= X_1 \cos \omega t.
\end{equation}
